The figures of the 739 open clusters that report in our paper (Cataloging Distant Galactic Open Clusters: Identification of  739 New Star Clusters Beyond 5 kpc Utilizing GAIA DR3 Data) complete King's model  profile fitting, sky charts and 5-panels ( spatial distribution, proper-motion distribution, parallax statistics, parallax distribution, and CMD) .

File of "cross_match_venn.zip" includes the 739 VENN diagrams of open cluster candidates.
